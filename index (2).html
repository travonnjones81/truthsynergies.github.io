Mission

Truth Changes Things.

TRUTH Synergies NFP is a ministry of human, business, and community development rooted in the Metro East region of Illinois.

Through trauma-informed training, community accountability, and practical personal development, we help people rebuild identity, strengthen decision-making, and build lives that make their neighborhoods safer and stronger.

We don't run programs at communities. We build capacity within them.

Trauma-Informed Training

Programs designed around the real conditions people carry, not the idealized versions funders prefer.

Leadership Development

Equipping residents to lead, not just participate. The community produces its own solutions.

Violence Prevention

Crisis readiness, de-escalation, and community accountability as foundations for lasting safety.

Economic Progression

Workforce readiness and entrepreneurship support for people navigating major life transitions.

Flagship Program

Community Guardian System

A locally-driven community resilience model that prepares residents to stabilize crises, prevent violence, and build the conditions necessary for long-term prosperity. We start with a community risk assessment, then build a customized training suite to match.

  • Conflict de-escalation
  • Overdose response with Narcan
  • Trauma first aid and bleeding control
  • Mental health crisis stabilization
  • Civic engagement and nonviolent organizing

About

Rooted Here.
Built for This.

TraVonn Jones founded TRUTH Synergies NFP after years of seeing what communities in the Metro East actually need versus what outside programs deliver. He grew up in these communities. His authority comes from that, as much as from his professional training and practice.

A retired United States Marine, Jones specializes in experiential facilitation and program design that connects practical skill development with leadership formation. He has worked with communities across Illinois including Little Village and Austin in Chicago and Peoria, helping develop locally-driven strategies that strengthen community stability and long-term economic mobility.

TRUTH operates at the intersection of trauma-informed workforce development, violence prevention, leadership development, and community stabilization. The work is grounded in lived experience, faith-centered leadership, and practical systems thinking.

TraVonn Jones Founder & President, TRUTH Synergies NFP  ·  USMC Veteran
